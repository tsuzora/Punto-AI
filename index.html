HOW TO PLAY

1. Objective

Be the first to create a straight line of 4 cards of your color (Horizontal, Vertical, or Diagonal).

2. Placing Cards

Place a card from your hand adjacent to any existing card on the board.

3. Overwriting (Steal)

You can place a card ON TOP of an opponent's card if your card has a higher value.

4. Constraints

The board cannot grow larger than 6x6 cells. Moves that exceed this size are invalid.
